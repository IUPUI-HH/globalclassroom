Behind the curtains!

The creators of this website on Intercultural Communication in a Global Classroom are a combination of two students from IUPUI in the United States and two students from Haaga-Helia in Finland. Though in different locations and different time zones, these students were able to communicate and collaborate their times to work together to create this website.

Note: The views, opinions, and information provided on this website concerning Intercultural Communication are those provided by the creators of the website themselves. These creators are not professionals or experts on the topic of Intercultural Communication.
